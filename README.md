# Azin Game - RPG Mod

Welcome to **Azin Game**, an immersive RPG experience with a wide array of features that will keep you entertained for hours. In this mod, players can explore a vast open world, engage in intense battles, customize their characters, and interact with other players in various ways. Please note that **Azin Game** is an older project, with the last update being in **2020**. While it's a classic, it may not feature the latest advancements in gaming technology.

---

## Key Features

### **Admin Levels**
- **13 Admin Levels**: Enjoy a comprehensive system with **13 admin ranks**, offering various privileges and powers to the admins for managing the server.
- **5 Helper Levels**: A dedicated tier system with **5 helper levels**, making it easy for players to find support and assistance from the game staff.

### **BOSS System**
- **Boss NPCs**: Every few hours, a special **BOSS NPC** spawns, and players can band together to defeat it for exciting rewards. These bosses are challenging and provide high-stakes gameplay!

### **Toys System**
- **Toys & Accessories**: Players can customize their characters with **toys** and **accessories**, offering a fun way to personalize their experience in-game.

### **Smartphone System**
- **In-game Smartphones**: Players can own and use **smartphones** to access various features, contact other players, or track game events.

### **Email & Phone Verification**
- **Security Systems**: The game has robust **email and phone verification** systems to ensure player authenticity and prevent unauthorized access.

### **Anti-Cheat Protection**
- **Strong Anti-Cheat**: We've integrated an advanced **anti-cheat** system to ensure a fair and competitive environment for all players.

### **Betting System**
- **Betting Zones**: A **betting system** opens at specific times of the day, where players can engage in wagers with each other in a safe and controlled environment.

### **Prison Escape System**
- **Escape from Jail**: Experience the thrill of trying to **escape from prison** in a custom-designed facility, or face the consequences of your actions!

### **Event System**
- **New and Exciting Events**: Participate in regular **events** that bring new and exciting challenges to the game. From limited-time quests to huge in-game celebrations, there's always something happening.

### **Car Tuning**
- **Car Customization**: Take your vehicle to the next level with **advanced car tuning** systems, allowing you to personalize and modify your cars with a wide range of options.

### **A Coin System**
- **A Coin**: Similar to Bitcoin, the **A Coin** fluctuates in value and can be mined, traded, and used for purchases in the game. Be strategic with your investments as the market can go up or down.

### **Turf Wars & Mafia**
- **Mafia Factions**: Join or create your own **mafia factions** and claim territory on the map. Engage in **turf wars** to dominate the world of Azin and take control of valuable lands.

### **Bs Mat & Buff System**
- **Bs Mat**: Defeat bosses to earn **Bs Mat** and convert it into **Buffs** that give your character powerful abilities and advantages for battle.

### **Admin Rank Purchase**
- **Hoster Rank**: Players can purchase the **Hoster Admin Rank** through the game’s **shop**, granting them exclusive powers and privileges to enhance their gameplay experience.

### **Unbanning with Gold**
- **Ban System**: Players who have been banned can purchase their **unban** by using **Gold**, an in-game currency, giving them a second chance to re-enter the game.

### **Safezones**
- **Safezones**: Numerous **safezones** are scattered across the map to prevent unnecessary player conflicts in certain areas. These areas provide a safe space for players to relax, trade, or regroup.

### **Amusement Park System**
- **Amusement Park**: Take a break from the chaos and enjoy the **amusement park**, featuring thrilling rides and activities to relax and unwind.

### **Skin Shop**
- **Skin Customization**: Visit the **Skin Shop** to purchase a variety of **skins** to further customize your character's look.

### **Bank Heist System**
- **Bank Robbery**: Plan and execute **bank robberies** with other players. The heists can be done solo or in groups, and successful robberies result in massive payouts! But beware: the police will be after you, and the stakes are high. Be prepared for intense action and strategy.

### **Additional Features**
- **Vehicle Purchases**: Buy, sell, and upgrade a wide range of **vehicles** to cruise around the game world in style.
- **Factions & Jobs**: Join various **factions**, take on exciting **jobs**, or even engage in **bank heists**. There's always something to do in the vast world of Azin Game!

## Getting Started

### **How to Join the Server**
1. Install the **Azin Game mod** on your platform (installation instructions will be provided in the mod files).
2. Join the **official Azin Game server**.
3. Create your character and start exploring the world of Azin!
   
### **Gameplay Tips**
- **Team Up for Boss Fights**: The **Boss NPCs** are tough, so it’s best to team up with other players to increase your chances of success.
- **Mine A Coins**: If you're interested in cryptocurrencies, **A Coin** mining can be a lucrative way to gain in-game wealth. Keep an eye on market trends!
- **Explore Turf Wars**: Become part of a **mafia faction**, dominate territories, and participate in **turf wars** to grow your faction's power.
- **Master Bank Heists**: Plan carefully and execute a **bank robbery** to secure your fortune. Be strategic and avoid getting caught!
